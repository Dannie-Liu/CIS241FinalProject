ABOUT ME

Hello Readers! My name is Yadan Liu, but here in the U.S.I go by Dannie. Originally from China, I moved to Omaha, Nebraska to earn my Master's Degree in Rehabilitation Sciences. After earning the degree, I decided to move to Houston, Texas for better access to jobs. Within a week, I was fully packed and on a southbound flight. Two days later, I had a job working in an Occupational Therapy clinic.

I didn't plan on staying too long. I figured that after a couple years, my adventure would come to an end, and I would move back to China better prepared for my career. But fate had other plans because after only two months in Houston, I met the man who is now my husband. 5 years later, we're still here and building our life together. But I'm getting ahead of myself.

While working at the Occupational Therapy clinic, I was inspired to pursue an Associate's Degree in an Occupational Therapy Assistant program in Houston. While studying there, I participated in a seminar about Assistive Technology, and I decided would use the new degree and previous healthcare experience to test into the field. I passed my exams and got certified as both an Occupational Therapy Assistant and an Assistive Technology Professional shortly after my wedding was held in the final days before Covid lockdowns began.

Looking for a new adventure with my husband, we decided to start looking for jobs in the Seattle area. We've been in Washington now for almost two years, and it's definitely had its ups and downs. My husband landed a great tech job, we bought a townhome together, and just a month ago, I gave birth to our first child. On the other hand, I was laid off in February, a week before I found out I was pregnant. But after talking it out with my husband and seeing the success his career has brought him and after I took a few programming courses on Udemy, I decided I would use my newfound freedom to study web development.

I enjoy hiking in Washington State and traveling with my family as much as I can. while traveling, I also like to take some beautiful pictures.

And here, dear readers, do you find me now. I am passionately studying away for what may well become my greatest adventure yet (okay, maybe second to motherhood). I'm excited to have you along as part of my journey!
